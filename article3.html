LeBron James

Who is LeBron James?

LeBron Raymone James is an American basketball player born on December 30, 1984, in Akron, Ohio. He skipped college to join the NBA's Cleveland Cavaliers.

Nickname: King James

Education: St. Vincent-St. Mary High School

Achievements

He first gets the national attention as the top high school basketball player in his country. With his unique combination of size and athleticism he received National Basketball Association Most Valuable Player (NBA MVP) Award for four-times in his sports life.

During the 2003-04, he made a history by becoming the first member of Cavalier franchise to win the NBA Rookie of the Year Award. Also, after leading the Miami Heat to titles in 2012 and 2013, James returned to Cleveland and helped the franchise claim its first championship in 2016. Again in 2005, he became the youngest player ever to score more than 50 points in a single game, increasing his average to 27.2.
